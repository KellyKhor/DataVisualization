European GDP Trends (2013-2023)

An Interactive Year-by-Year Comparison Across Countriess

This bar chart effectively illustrates GDP across European countries from 2013 to 2023, with a slider enabling users to explore year-by-year data. The y-axis represents GDP in USD, and the x-axis shows the countries. A bar chart is particularly useful here because the varying bar heights offer a clear visual comparison of GDP values across countries, making differences immediately visible.
